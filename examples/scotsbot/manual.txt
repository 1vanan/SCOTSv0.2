Scotsbot - The Implementations of SCOTS on Khepera-IV 


Directions for files:


I. bot_scots.cc - Code for implementing the recurrence specification with two targets sets on the platform

Requires reach.scs and reach2.scs as the look-up tables for the respective target sets


II. bot_scots_reach.cc - Code for implementing the reachability specification with two targets sets on the platform

Requires reach.scs as the look-up table for the target set.


All .hh files are the headers included in the I and II


Note: I and II requires libraries and cross-compiler for the bot.

Manual for installation of the libraries and cross-compiler: 

http://ftp.k-team.com/KheperaIV/UserManual/Khepera%20IV%20User%20Manual.pdf
